bops for working but actually for distracting yourself

I created an interactive music visualization map that turns my listening history into a dynamic chain of color-coded blocks. Each block represents a song, with its length corresponding to the song's duration and its width reflecting how hyped I felt while listening. This project is my way of capturing both the time and emotion behind the songs I love in a visually engaging experience.
