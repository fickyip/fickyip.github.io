Evviva

The design brief aims to create a packaging design for a brand. The objective is to create a design that not only ensures structural integrity and consumer convenience but also serves as a compelling physical manifestation of the brand’s identity.

“Evviva” an Italian interjection meaning hurray. Food is the most profound way we celebrate the gift of life. A meal is never just mere sustenance, it is a vibrant ceremony of flavor, culture, and connection that turns the act of eating into a festive event.
